# Amazon-Sales-Data-Analysis-Project
## Project Overview

This project involves analyzing Amazon sales data to extract insights related to product pricing, discount behavior, customer reviews, and purchasing preferences. The analysis focuses on various aspects of product performance, such as filtering by price, identifying discount ranges, studying review text, and evaluating category-based trends. These insights support data-driven decisions in pricing strategy, marketing, and product improvement.

## Questions Covered in the Analysis

List all products with a discounted price below ₹500.

Find products with a discount percentage of 50% or more.

Retrieve all products where the name contains the word "Cable".

Display the difference between the average actual price and average discounted price for each product.

Query reviews that mention "fast charging".

Identify products with a discount percentage between 20% and 40%.

Find products that have an actual price above ₹1,000 and are rated 4 stars or above.

Find products where the discounted price ends with 9.

Display reviews containing negative words like worst, waste, poor, or not good.

List all products where the category includes "Accessories".

## Conclusion

The Amazon Sales Analysis provides significant insights into product performance, customer preferences, and pricing effectiveness. Key findings highlight the impact of high discount offers, the importance of product features like fast charging, and the role of customer reviews in shaping product perception.

By applying these insights—such as optimizing discount strategies, improving product quality, and addressing negative feedback—Amazon can enhance customer satisfaction and boost sales. Additionally, understanding customer behavior through review patterns and pricing trends supports stronger decision-making in marketing, inventory management, and product development.
